About Me

Welcome to my blog! I'm Ethan Babb, a graduate of Washington and Lee University with a degree in Economics and Accounting. My academic background has given me a strong foundation in understanding markets, financial systems, and economic trends.

Through this blog, I share my thoughts, analyses, and observations on topics I'm passionate about, including real estate markets, urban planning initiatives, business developments, technology trends (especially AI), college football, and travel experiences.

My goal is to create a space where I can engage with these topics critically, share interesting insights, and connect with others who share similar interests.
